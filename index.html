Music Library

How to Use This Website

  1. Go to Upload Songs to add your music files to the library.
  2. Click on a song in the library to play it instantly.
  3. Create and manage your own playlists in the Playlists section.
  4. Use the bottom navigation to quickly move between Home, Upload, and Playlists.
  5. Enjoy your music anywhere and anytime directly from the web app!
